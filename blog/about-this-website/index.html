About This Website

December 26, 2023 / 1 min read

Hello, and welcome to my personal website! I invite you to explore this blog and get to know me better. My website is built using the Myour theme developed by bslthemes, and I’ve utilized Hugo to deploy it as a static website hosted on GitHub Pages.

I created this website because I believe that mainstream academic profiles often provide limited insights into a person’s complete identity. I wanted people to have a more holistic understanding of me as an individual, which is why, in addition to my academic profile, I built this personal website.

It’s important to note that the “me” you encounter on this website is not the entirety of who I am. While I’ve made every effort to reflect my authentic self on the website, it’s inevitable that subconscious self-presentation may influence how I come across. So, regardless of your purpose for visiting this website, I encourage you to maintain a critical perspective and avoid conflating the image of me you perceive while browsing with my true self.

Enjoy your exploration, and thank you for visiting!
